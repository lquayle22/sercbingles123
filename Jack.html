What to do Bangor?

Bangor Marina

Bangor Marina offers well maintained pontoon berths which are encompassed by extensive breakwaters making the marina unique in offering sheltered, safe and secure berthing. Bangor Marina is open and manned 24/7/365 by a friendly, helpful team who understand boat owner needs.

Karting

A benefit of go-karting in Bangor is that it’s possible to do it with everyone. The venue of Eddie Irvine Sports is suitable for every age. It doesn’t matter if you’re young, old, an experienced driver, or going karting for the first time, everyone is ensured to have a good time.

Bar Crawl

Pub crawls in Bangor are quite easy partially because most of the pubs and clubs are located down the sea front, and you can roll from one to the one next door a lot of the time.Bangor has a wide varity of pubs and clubs to choose from if your interested in a bar crawl.
